Overview

Inbox Cleaner reads your last 50 Gmail messages, runs each through an AI classifier that distinguishes spam, phishing, and promotions from legitimate mail, then automatically moves junk to Trash — while simultaneously logging every trashed email to a Google Sheet for your records.

How It Works

📬 Gmail Reader Reads last 50 inbox emails · extracts subject, sender, body, date, ID
↓
🤖 AI Junk Classifier Analyses urgency tactics · suspicious senders · grammar · prize scams · trusted contacts
Routes each email to one of two paths
🗑️ Junk
🗑️ Bulk Trash Moves all junk emails to Gmail Trash using sender + subject
simultaneously
📊 Log to Google Sheet Subject · Sender · Date logged as a permanent record
✅ Legitimate
✅ Left Untouched Receipts, confirmations, personal & professional emails stay in inbox

Step-by-Step Breakdown

1
Gmail Reader
Fetches the 50 most recent inbox emails, extracting subject, sender, body, date, and message ID for processing.
2
AI Junk Classifier (Router)
Each email is scored by AI against spam signals — urgency language, suspicious domains, grammar issues, prize scams — and routed to Junk or Legitimate.
3
Bulk Trash (Junk Route)
All junk emails are moved to Gmail Trash in bulk using their sender addresses and subjects.
4
Log to Google Sheets (Simultaneous)
In parallel with trashing, every junk email is logged to a Google Sheet — Subject, Sender, Date — creating a full audit trail.
