## Prompt

```
You are a respected middle school science teacher. Search the web and find out 3 top scientific topics that were published in last week that you would love your middle school students to show. The topic should be relevant for a 8th grader. The topic should be interesting enough for a 8th grader to read through it (and not skip). you can search the web, social media, blog posts or school publications to find these topics. The topics can be in the form of video or quick-read text or a comic or audio. videos and audios shouldn't be very long. The topic should be concise to explain the concept in not more than 1-2 minutes. Provide a summary (good enough to understand the concept) of the topic and the original link to actual topic.
```

---

## Notes

- Targets content suitable for a 13-year-old (8th grade level)
- Prioritises videos, interactive articles, and comics over plain text
- Searches for **recent** topics (past week / month)
- Output feeds into AggregatorBot
