Overview

DadBot runs 5 AI agents in parallel every week — 4 specialized content curator bots scour the web simultaneously, then a final aggregator bot picks the best from each and formats everything into a beautiful HTML email digest delivered straight to the inbox.

How It Works

📅 Weekly Schedule Trigger
↓
Runs 4 bots simultaneously
🔬 ScienceBot 3 science topics for 8th graders
💡 InspireBot 3 life lessons & quotes
😄 JokeBot 3 age-appropriate dad jokes
🌟 GoodNewsBot 3 uplifting news stories
↓
🧩 AggregatorBot Picks 1 best from each · Builds HTML email
↓
📧 "DadBot Weekly Digest" Delivered to inbox every week

The 4 Content Curator Bots

🔬
ScienceBot
Searches the web for 3 recent science topics — engaging for 8th graders (videos, articles, comics)
💡
InspireBot
Finds 3 recent inspirational life lessons or quotes to build character, curiosity & resilience
😄
JokeBot
Searches for 3 recent dad jokes appropriate for a 13-year-old, prioritizing comics & pictures
🌟
GoodNewsBot
Curates 3 uplifting stories from the past week — kindness, breakthroughs, animal rescues, positive news
